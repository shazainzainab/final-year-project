Overview

Description

Como, Italy is a stunning lakeside city nestled beside the crystal-clear waters of Lake Como, surrounded by scenic mountains. Famous for its charming old town, historic cathedral, and luxury villas, it’s a perfect blend of nature and elegance.

History

Como’s history dates back to Roman times, when it was known as Novum Comum, a thriving settlement with ancient walls and structures. Over centuries, it flourished as a center of trade, silk production, and aristocratic villas along its famous lake.
